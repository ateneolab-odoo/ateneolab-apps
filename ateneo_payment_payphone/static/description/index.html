Get paid for your online selling products easily.

Easy settings screen

Configure your payment method in a bit

Go to Invoicing -> Configuration -> Payments ->Payment acquirers. Select and edit Payphone Payment acquirer. You need to provide URL Payphone and Token Payphone. You can also configure messages to show users how to go through the payment process.

Then just press the button!

Once you have decided your purchase, press Pay Now button.
